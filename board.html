Board Members

Mohamed Ragheb

Mohamed Ragheb brings extensive technological expertise and educational leadership to the board. As a Senior Director and a current board member of the Ohio Public Library Information Network, he offers valuable insights into complex information systems. His past experience includes network engineering, IT administration, and teaching. Mohammed's dedication to education is further demonstrated by his years of service at ISNEO including Sunday School Principal and board member. He holds a Master's in Computer Technology and is responsible for IT and technology oversight, along with curriculum and instruction development for the school.

Ahmed Cheema

Ahmed Cheema is a highly skilled professional with a strong background in technology and education. As a Senior Software Engineer and an Area Director for a tutoring services business, he offers a unique blend of technical acumen and practical educational experience. His past roles include several years in systems analysis, programming, and data engineering. Ahmed, who holds a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering, oversees communication and technology initiatives for the school.

Eissa Attar

Eissa Attar provides a wealth of experience in finance, community engagement, and business management. He is an active member of Canton for All People and runs a catering business, demonstrating his commitment to community service and entrepreneurial spirit. Eissa also serves as Treasurer on the board of Masjid Al Rahman of Canton. With decades of experience in finance, marketing, and community projects, and a Master's in Business Management and Administration, Eissa is responsible for the school's HR, marketing, and financial aspects.

Ramzi Ksouri

Ramzi Ksouri brings practical expertise in facility management and construction to the board. As the owner of an automotive tire and light repair services facility, he has a strong understanding of operational oversight. His background includes several years of technical expertise in mechanical and automotive fields. With college coursework in business administration, Ramzi will have oversight over facility and construction-related needs for the school.
